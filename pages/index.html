Game Photography

If you have been using Steam's nifty screenshots feature or simply print screening, we want to be able to share your captured works of art with the world. Glorious albums of eternal graphics.

Crystal Curse: His memory graces us with another stunning screenshot from Dark Souls. The curvature of the ground makes it seem like he's standing on a crystalline world.
Head South: This guy looks into his compass as the sky opens up to shine light on a fading world. He might be low on blood, but in DayZ, that's just part of life.
Raceway: Codemasters has made some great-looking, playing, and sounding games. K-putt seems to agree as this amazing screenshot shows. The cloudy sky and spectators act as an appropriate backdrop to the rally car and it all makes you feel like you're actually there.
Alan Wake's American Nightmare isn't usually what we'd describe as graphics photorealism, but this particular shot is drop-dead gorgeous. Story might be the game's main selling point, but clearly, it's got the looks as well as the brains. Thanks again to K-putt.
